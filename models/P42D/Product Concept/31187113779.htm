The new MURANO is one of Nissan’s middle class crossover lines. The new MURANO is meant to be driven on the city streets, unlike Nissan’s PATHFINDER that is intended for off-road usage.

The crossover is defined as vehicles that have an SUV-like appearance but are built on a sedan-based platform. It combines sedan and SUV characteristics to create a unique vehicle that meets specific performance and lifestyle needs.
MURANO shares a platform with Altima and 2009 Maxima (Available only in the US). They contrast with Pathfinder, which is built on versions of Nissan’s body-on-frame truck platform.

The crossover has both following features and benefits of a sedan and SUV.
- Benefits of Sedan
Handling, Ride quality, Maneuverability, Exterior design, Fuel economy and cost
- Benefits of SUV
Off-road capability, Seating and interior utility, Cargo capacity and versatility
