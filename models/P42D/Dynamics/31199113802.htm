Concept
The new MURANO provides Quiet and Comfort, Agile and Linear Acceleration, and Easy and Reliable Handling during driving.

Dynamically, the main objective for the new MURANO is to evolve the sportiness DNA of the Z50 and improve the riding comfort to a midsize premium crossover.

- When driving in the city, the light steering provides a responsive handling and a comfortable ride.
- On the highway, the stable steering feel is a safety and responsive vehicle in motion..
- While changing lanes, the linear and smooth responsive acceleration makes agile and comfortable driving experience.
- Exiting the highway, the accurate and controllable brake action provides drivers with an overall safety feeling.
