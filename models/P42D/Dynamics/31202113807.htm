VDC
VDC enhances the stability by controlling the breaking pressure and reduces lots of driver‘s burden, with the combination of ALL MODE 4x4-i

To understand the schematic of each function, click 1, 2 and 3.

1. Over steer scenario
Decrease the yaw moment by applying the brake pressure for the outside tires and keep the target line.

2. Under steer scenario
Increase the yaw moment by applying the brake pressure for the inside tires and keep the target line.

3. On a slippery road surface
Move the driving force to high-µ side by braking to low-µ side and keep the target line.

VDC can reduce engine output and/or apply appropriate brake pressure to specific wheels that will help avoid any harmful collisions

* VDC is equivalent to ESP and ECS.
VDC : Vehicle Dynamics Control, ESP : Electronic Stability Program, ESC : Electronic Stability Control
