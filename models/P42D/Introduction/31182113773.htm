Course Overview
Nissan will apply a blended learning approach to target two major end-user audience groups:

Group 1: Salespersons who attended classroom training and require additional time to review the information on the new MURANO
1-1. Prerequisite Training:
Prerequisite training is required to be taken before attending the classroom training session.

Group 2: Salespersons who did not attend the classroom training:
2-1. Self-study Training:
Self-study training is only performed when you are unable to attend the classroom training session.

The definitions for each type of trainings are below.

1-2. Classroom Training: Classroom training is an instructor-led training session to obtain additional knowledge and details through discussion and role-play activities.
1-3. Post-study Training:
Post-study training is required to be taken after attending the classroom training session.
